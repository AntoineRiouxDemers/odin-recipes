Chinese Macaroni

Description

Chinese macaroni is a dish that incorporates macaroni pasta in Chinese cuisine.
It typically features Chinese flavors and ingredients, making it distinct from Western-style macaroni dishes.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni pasta
  • Vegetables (such as bell peppers, carrots, and peas)
  • Protein (chicken, pork, or shrimp)
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Garlic and ginger (for flavor)
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onions (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a wok or large pan, heat some oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and ginger to the hot oil and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add the protein (chicken, pork, or shrimp) and cook until browned and cooked through.
  5. Add the chopped vegetables and stir-fry until they are tender but still crisp.
  6. Toss in the cooked macaroni pasta and mix well with the other ingredients.
  7. Season the dish with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and a dash of sesame oil. Adjust the seasoning to taste.
  8. Continue to stir-fry until everything is well combined and heated through.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
